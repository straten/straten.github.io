Professional Information

I am currently a Senior Lecturer in the School of Engineering, Computer and Mathematical Sciences at Auckland University of Technology, working in the Institute for Radio Astronomy & Space Research.

My research interests include

  • high-precision pulsar timing, pulsar timing arrays, and low-frequency gravitational radiation;
  • radio polarimetry, single-pulse studies, and the pulsar emission mechanism;
  • the galactic magnetic field and pulsar Faraday rotation measurement;
  • interstellar scintillation and the cyclostationary statistics of the electromagnetic radiation from pulsars;
  • instrumentation and software, particularly digital signal processing on multi-core architectures; and
  • the exploitation of machine learning and autonomous agents in the search for new pulsars.
