Willem van Straten

Professional Information

For half of my time, I undertake research in astronomy with the team at Manly Astrophysics. For the other half of my time, I develop signal processing software for the Square Kilometre Array in my role as Chief Scientist at Fourier Space.

My research interests include

  • high-precision pulsar timing, pulsar timing arrays, and low-frequency gravitational radiation;
  • radio polarimetry, single-pulse studies, and the pulsar emission mechanism;
  • the galactic magnetic field and pulsar Faraday rotation measurement;
  • interstellar scintillation and the cyclostationary statistics of the electromagnetic radiation from pulsars; and
  • instrumentation and software, particularly digital signal processing on multi-core architectures.
